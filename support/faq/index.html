What is the developer+ program?

developer+ is a program to introduce developers to Rackspace, the Rackspace Cloud, managed services, and Fanatical Support® via developer-to-developer support. The program also helps developers to grow with Rackspace and to experience, first hand, the Managed Cloud.

Fundamentally, the developer+ program is an incubator for your idea. It provides an Infrastructure Credit, and period of time, so you can experience the full portfolio of Rackspace's cloud and build out your idea or business. Want to test out an MVP web application? You can do that. How about Experience CoreOS + Docker? You can do that as well (our CoreOS images are updated weekly). We want to provide you with what you need to build out modern applications on the world's largest OpenStack™ powered cloud.

Additionally, developer+ includes several direct feedback mechanisms (as part of the terms and conditions of signup) that allow our teams to get direct, honest feedback from customers. This is key to the development of the program.

For additional information, see the official announcement.

What is included in the developer+™ program?

Participants in the developer+ program:

  • receive an $50 infrastructure credit for 12 months that applies to all Rackspace Cloud services
  • do not pay a Service Level Rate or Service level minimum.
In addition, developer+ customers have access to other great free cloud services, like:
  • Cloud DNS
  • Cloud Orchestration
  • Cloud Monitoring
  • Cloud Networking
  • Mailgun™ (first 50,000 emails free)
and more being added all the time!

Some support options in the customer portal have been replaced for developer+ customers, such as one-to-one chat, support tickets, and the phone number. Tickets have been replaced with Billing Inquiries.

As new products and services come online, they may be added to, and highlighted in, this program.

How is developer+ different than Managed Infrastructure/Operations?

Customers in the developer+ program are a part of the Managed Infrastructure service level, but developer+ have access to phone and ticket support for billing/account related issues only. Most of our developers are self supporting, or use our community resourcs like those detailed in a How do I get support?

Are the services rate limited?

No, any rate limits that apply are our documented default service limits for all Rackspace customers. We have not throttled, crippled, or otherwise made this a technically-inferior program for developers.

You have complete access to all services and features.

How do I get support?

Members of our developer+ program have access to many free and powerful self-support resources:
  • The Rackspace Developer forum at https://community.rackspace.com/developers/default
  • #rackspace on Freenode IRC
  • Twitter: @rackspace
  • For SDK and tools questions, you can mail us at sdk-support@rackspace.com
  • Knowledge Center
  • Community resources like Stackoverflow, that we participate in regularly

Please see Developer-to-Developer Support for more info.

Note: Tickets filed in the Cloud Control Panel are limited to billing inquires only.

What if developers want to add another server? Test a website or database?

developer+ customers can consume cloud at a "pay for what you consume" competitive prices for all services in any region. They can consume ObjectRocket™, OnMetal™, and everything else currently available to our other customers.

Can developer+ participants upgrade to another service level?

At any time, participants in the program may log into the Control Panel and upgrade to our Managed Cloud service levels. Customers may not, however, switch to developer+.

back to top

Can existing customers have their accounts moved to developer+?

Not at this time. Only new accounts can choose the developer+ program.

back to top

How are developer+ customers billed?

Customers in developer+ pay only for infrastructure they consume over $50 (30 GBP) each month for 12 months.

back to top

What happens at the end of 12 months?

Customers in developer+ will be contacted prior to the 12 month term completion to discuss upgrading to the Managed Infrastructure or Managed Operations service levels. By default, at the end of 12 months the Managed Infrastructure level will be applied including Service Level rates.

Are developer+ customers eligible for the SLA guarantees covered by Managed Infrastructure?

Yes, but their credit is reduced by 50 percent as noted in the Terms and Conditions.
